Why Weekend Farming?

Eco-Friendly

Help reduce carbon footprint by practicing sustainable farming.

Healthy Lifestyle

Enjoy fresh air, exercise, and grow organic food.

Build Community

Meet like-minded individuals and share farming experiences.

Weekend Farming Opportunities

Farm Fresh Food

Enjoy fresh, organic food straight from the farm to your table.

Farm Stay

Experience a relaxing weekend stay at a farm surrounded by nature.

Puddling in Paddy

Take part in traditional paddy field puddling and enjoy rural life.

Cycling

Explore the farm and surrounding areas on a leisurely cycling tour.

Still Not Convinced?

Discover the countless benefits of contributing and joining hands for a better future:

  • Escape from the chaos of daily life and reconnect with nature.
  • Savor the goodness of farm-fresh, organic food straight from the source.
  • Support sustainable farming and help local communities thrive.
  • Be a part of creating opportunities for underprivileged families.
  • Experience the joy of making a difference in someone's life.

Wanna See Them Smiling?

Your contribution can bring joy and hope to countless lives. Funding ensures better resources, education, and opportunities for those in need. Let's make a difference together—because every smile counts!
